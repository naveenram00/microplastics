Possible Solutions

A few possible solutions have been proposed to the microplastics issue. Scientist are not in agreement about the best way to combat the problem. One proposed solution includes gathering the plastics into a floating, walled-off section of the ocean, where sea life can pass underneath. Some argue that our main focus needs to be in reducing plastic production and finding viable alternatives to synthetic materials. Hopefully as research continues, we will be better aquiped to solve the issue.
